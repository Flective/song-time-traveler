**WHICH THEME IS RIGHT FOR YOU?**

Answer these questions:

1. **Darkness preference?**
   - Very dark → Neon Synthwave or Platonic Ideal
   - Dark → Noir Jazz or Broken Light
   - Light → Ethereal Minimal

2. **Energy level?**
   - High energy → Neon Synthwave
   - Medium → Broken Light
   - Calm → Noir Jazz, Ethereal Minimal, Platonic Ideal

3. **Brand personality?**
   - Sophisticated → Noir Jazz
   - Futuristic → Neon Synthwave
   - Authentic → Broken Light
   - Elegant → Ethereal Minimal
   - Intellectual → Platonic Ideal

4. **Primary use?**
   - Jazz/sophisticated music → Noir Jazz
   - Electronic/synth music → Neon Synthwave
   - Rock/alternative music → Broken Light
   - Folk/intimate music → Ethereal Minimal
   - Documentation/framework → Platonic Ideal
