About

Written by hand,
across decades.

Words and music by James Pauli, working in the American popular tradition. Many of these songs waited years — on cassette, in notebooks — before finding the productions they were always reaching toward.

Each rendering carries a vocal guide track by James Pauli, modified by AI, under his direction at every step. The compositions are his; the technology only helps them arrive.

A song, here, is not a single recording. It is a form you can walk around — heard from several angles, each catching a different light. More songs, more triangulations, and the first music videos are on their way.
