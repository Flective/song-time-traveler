About This Catalog

Human-written.
AI-produced.
Clearly yours.

Every composition in this catalog was written by James Pauli — a songwriter working in the American popular tradition — between 1982 and 1998. The songs lived on cassette tape for decades before being produced using a Human-in-the-Loop AI methodology: AI as instrument, human as creative director, at every step.

This is not a prompt-generated catalog. There is a songwriter of record. There is a chain of title. There are no sample clearances. Ownership is unambiguous, which means your clearance process is straightforward.

Because production is AI-assisted, custom edits, alternate productions, and tempo or key variants are available within 48 hours of request — something no traditional catalog can offer at scale.
