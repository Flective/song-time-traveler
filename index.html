The Idea

Walk around the song.

A statue looks one way from the front and another from the side. No single view is the "real" one — the work is the whole of them, held together in the mind. Songs, we believe, are the same.

So each song here begins with a single — a clear way in, the cut you'd release. But the single is only one angle. Its triangulations turn the same composition toward other light: more intimate, more experimental, more performed. Play the single, then switch. The song you assemble in your own ear is the truest one of all.
